Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian dish enjoyed the world over. It consists of a creamy sauce made with eggs and hard cheese, with cured meat such as pancetta or bacon lardons and black pepper.

Discover how to make this tasty dish in the comfort of your own home with these easy steps.

Ingredients

  • 100g of pancetta (bacon lardons if pancetta is not available)
  • 50g of pecorino cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g parmesan
  • 350g of spaghetti
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g of butter
  • sea salt and black pepper

Method

  1. put a large sauce pan of water to boild
  2. thinly slice the pancetta or lardons, finely grate the parmesan and pecorino and mix them togther
  3. whisk the eggs in a bowl, add the black pepper then set aside
  4. add salt to the boiling water, add the spaghetti to the water and simmer until the pasta is al dente (usually 10 mins)
  5. crush the garlic cloves with a knife, and while the spaghetti is cooking fry the pancetta with the garlic in the butter. Cook for around 5 mins stirring often until the pancetta is crisp. Remove the garlic cloves
  6. continue to cook the pancetta on a low heat, when the past is cooked at it to the pan with the pancetta, be sure to keep the pasta water, it will be needed later
  7. Mix the eggs with the cheese, but sure to keep some cheese to top the dish later
  8. Next remove the spaghetti and pancetta from the heat, next add the eggs and cheese and stir into the pasta making sure all is coated
  9. add pasta water to the dish, making sure its not to wet, 2 tablespoons should do, add salt to taste
  10. serve immiediately, garnish with the set aside cheese, add extra salt and black pepper to taste
