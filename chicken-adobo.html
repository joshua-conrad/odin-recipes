Chicken Adobo

Description

A stable dish of the Philippines, it incorporates soy sauce,vinegar and sugar to create a richly falvoured dish with the taste of being slow cooked, however, ist not!

Adobo comes in many varities and uses different meats, for the recipe we shall focus on the classic chicken adobo

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1.5kg of chicken thighs
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tablespoons of crushed garlic
  • 2/3 of a cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 of a cup of white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

Method

  1. in a large pan heat the oil on a medium heat and brown the chicken all over, once done remove from the pan and set aside
  2. next dice the onion and at to the pan with the garlic and cook until soft and brown (5-6 minutes)
  3. next add the soy suace and the vinegar to the pan and season with the bay leaf, salt and black pepper
  4. finally, add the chicken back to the pan and bring to the boil, then reduce to a medium low heat and simmer until the chicken is tender (usually 35-45mins)
